About Me

Curious, with a proclivity for aesthetics
Mother of Four ◦ Veteran ◦ Bibliophile ◦ Chronic Sufferer of Wonderlust

If I were a cat, I'd be on maybe my fifth life. I am US Army Vet whom served in Operation Iraqi Freedom, mother of four. Before I found my love of code, I worked mostly in digital marketing and sales.

As a web developer and designer, my passion is mastering new and emerging technologies and sharing cool things I learn with my peers. I look forward to my growth and maturation as a developer and driven to use technology based solutions to help solve real world challenges.

When my head isn't buried in my laptop, my time is occupied with my four kids, watching Italian horror films, and eating good food. I am also quite the bibliophile and will read anything. I love Harry Potter as much as The Raven. Some side projects that I have now is developing a community outreach program through my blackGirlsCode(2) organization, and also writing a children's book with my eight-year-old daughter Amaya

Black Girls Code

bGC(2) exists to connect and build a community of women of color in technology. It's a safe place for members to learn new skills and grow in their careers. We come from different backgrounds, either self-taught or college educated, all with a unique story that has brings us together. What we all have in common is a burning desire to create our very own versions of disruptive technology.To learn more about what we have going on check us out on Meetup, or blackGirlsCode.github.io If you wish to contribute to any of our projects, find us on GitHub
