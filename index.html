About Me

ERICA MITCHELL

Founder, bGC(2) | Web Developer

If I were a cat, I"d be on maybe my fifth life. I am US Army Vet whom served in Operation Iraqi Freedom, and mother of four. Before I found my love of code, I worked mostly in digital marketing. As a web developer and designer, my passion is mastering new and emerging technologies and sharing cool things I learn with my peers. I look forward to my growth and maturation as a developer and driven to use technology based solutions to help solve real world challenges. When my head isn't buried in my laptop, my time is occupied with my four kids, watching Italian horror films, and eating good food. I am also quite the bibliophile and will read anything. I love Harry Potter as much as The Raven. Some side projects that I have now is developing a community outreach program through my blackGirlsCode(2) organization, and also writing a children's book with my eight-year-old daughter Amaya.

I created bGC(2) to connect women of color with a similar love for technology. It's a safe place for members to learn new skills and grow in their careers. We come from different backgrounds, either self-taught or college educated, all with a unique story that has brings us together. What we all have in common is a burning desire to create our very own versions of disruptive technology.To learn more about what we have going on check us out on Meetup, or blackGirlsCode.github.io If you wish to contribute, join or support our efforts.

Slight Werk - Pomodoro Clock

JavascriptResponsive Design

Javascript & JQuery was used to create a pomodoro clock. The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the late 1980s. It uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks..

    User Stories:
  1. I can start a 25 minute pomodoro, and the timer will go off once 25 minutes has elapsed.
  2. I can reset the clock for my next pomodoro.
  3. I can customize the length of each pomodoro.
  4. BONUS A diifferent color displays alerting time remaining in each pomodoro.
