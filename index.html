About blackGirlsCode(2)

blackGirlsCode(2) exists to connect and build a community of women of color in technology and close the gap in the digitial divide. It's a safe place for women of color to learn software development and grow in their careers. Our members come from different backgrounds, either self-taught or college educated, all with a unique story that has brought them together. The common thread that binds us is to seek likeness in another and a burning desire to create our very own versions of disruptive technology.

The vision of bGC(2) continually evolves as new members come for coffee && code, or when someone shares a stellar idea. If you are interested to learn more about what we have going on check us out on Meetup, or blackGirlsCode.github.io If you wish to contribute to any of our projects, find us on GitHub
